Features you will love

Easy Video Management

Miro Community lets you rapidly bring together video from any source into a beautiful video website. What’s most powerful is how quickly you can manage incoming sources. Add videos manually, accept user submissions, or connect accounts from Vimeo, YouTube, and RSS feeds. Any source can automatically be approved for your front page, or you can quickly manage the incoming queue and just publish the best ones. It’s simple the fastest way to curate a great video site.

Elegant, Powerful Presentation

Miro Community helps you make a gorgeous video site in seconds. Use one of our existing templates or customize your own theme with any style you design.

Integrate & Share

Miro Community can integrate with your own website — set it up at your own domain name or make it a section of your existing site. Built-in integration with Facebook, Twitter, and Google+ helps your content spread quickly.
