Managing the Explosive Growth of Media on Campus

Every university community is producing increasing amounts of media - each major department may be making their own videos — possibly on YouTube, Vimeo, or other hosting sites — along with student groups, video lectures on iTunes U, and media appearances of faculty and students.

At the same time, universities face an increasingly competitive environment that requires creative solutions in order to capture the attention of and connect with prospective students and continue to engage alumni and donors. And all of this, in a time of exploding social media options, when controlling brand has become extremely difficult as conversations are distributed across the web.

You may be starting to feel like you’re in the business of having to constantly chase down media from all sides or letting it fall through the cracks. Or that you’re becoming a technology company in order to keep up with the quickly changing media landscape and the increasing demands around media management within your institution.

How Does it Work?

Miro Community allows you to run a beautiful video presentation website on your own domain, without having to maintain the software. It has advanced curation and moderation functions, is highly customizable, and doesn’t take a lot of time to manage or run.

Great Benefits

  • Site appears at your own domain
  • Customizable with your school’s branding
  • Import videos from any video site
  • Moderate videos and comments
