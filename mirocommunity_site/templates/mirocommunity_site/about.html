Bring together video from across the web

Miro Community in a Nutshell

Most organizations don’t have a single outlet for videos, and with Miro Community that’s fine - you get the benefits of exposure on YouTube, iTunes U, and wherever else you publish, without needing to re-host centrally.

Miro Community gives you leverage: it’s a simple way to manage 10,000+ videos, no matter where they are hosted. You get a branded video portal that: is tailored to you, drives SEO directly to your site, and integrates with your existing web strategy.

Background

Miro Community began with a focus on building local video communities in a time when local news outlets were shrinking while more and more people and organizations turned to online video to present their grassroots experiences. With a generous grant from the Knight Foundation, the Participatory Culture Foundation set out to give any community a chance to provide a local portal to community video content. Along the way, Miro Community transformed into something much broader - a way for any community or organization to easily manage an ever increasing amount of relevant video and create focused spaces around their topic of interest.

Find the Miro Community Solution that’s right for you

Create a Hosted Site

Designed to be easy for anyone to create and maintain, with our freemium hosted service you get up to 25,000 videos and never have to worry about handling updates or maintenance. No special servers needed - embed videos from across the web. A light-weight solution for those with limited branding needs or their own design team.

Sign Up for Enterprise

We also offer enterprise services, where we’ll work with you to develop a branded custom theme for your site, provide a SLA, and even develop custom features as needed. This option includes unlimited videos. Enterprise sites are maintained on a private server to further protect against usage spikes, and are offered a private sandbox to test out updates and design changes.
